Where am I from?

I'm originally from Colombia, here are some random facts most people don't know about Colombia: Colombia is the second most biodiverse country in the world (plants, birds, insects, you name it). Bogota -the capital city- has South America’s largest network of bicycle routes. Colombia is on the equator, so expect almost no seasons and much heat and sun throughout the year! The country’s name is spelled Col-o-mbia, not Col-u-mbia. Many non-Spanish speakers get this wrong.
